  • The Environmental Collective

    About Us

    The Environmental Collective was founded in 2021 by two high school students in Surrey, British Columbia. We recognize the intersectionality of socioeconomic and ecological injustices in our society and are determined to push for greener solutions to drive systemic change.

    Vision & Mission

    Vision: A future where all Canadians actively cultivate a greener society by promoting sustainability and socioeconomic equality.
    Mission:To mitigate the effects of climate change and foster sustainability.
